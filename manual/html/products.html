Products

For two functions defined on the unit sphere, the pointwise product can be calculated directly in the basis of spherical harmonics using the Clebsch-Gordan coefficients. These coefficients describe the coupling between different bands and are a well-studied subject in physics. We calculate the Clebsch-Gordan coefficients as stated in Straub (2015) (see Literature).
