Pitfalls

Here we will show a list of known pitfalls, i.e. problems you might encounter which are not necessarily bugs.

  • Always get rid of all warnings before continuing, except you exactly know what you do!
  • Rotations are passed and returned in radians!
  • Study the Coordinate System carefully to avoid problems on reading panoramic images and rotating data. They are the same for all used types of data, e.g. shc::Shpm, Pointclouds, etc.
  • For constistency the one-sided tangent distance is always applied to the second Shpm argument passed to the functions shc::Shc::get_feature_difference and shc::Shc::compass.
